RouteFlow Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

RouteFlow (“we”, “us”) provides a group travel planning app. This policy describes how we handle information when you use RouteFlow.

What we collect

  • Account data: email and profile details you provide via sign-in (e.g. Apple or Google).
  • Trip content: trip names, places, expenses, community posts, and optional photos you add.
  • Diagnostics: crash and error reports via Sentry when enabled, to fix bugs.
  • Purchases: subscription status is processed by Apple, Google, and RevenueCat; we store plan status in our backend to unlock features.

How we use data

We use data to run the service, sync your trips across devices, show community features you choose to publish, and improve reliability.

Sharing

We use trusted processors (e.g. Supabase for database and auth, Google Places for place search, RevenueCat for subscriptions). We do not sell your personal information.

Your choices

You may sign out, delete trip content in-app where supported, or contact us to request account or data deletion where applicable by law.

Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@routeflow.app
